## Welcome to Gradison!

At Gradison, we are committed to providing you with a transparent and clear understanding of how we use cookies and similar technologies. This Cookies Policy outlines the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on the computers and mobile devices of people who visit those websites. They allow a website to remember your device and how you interacted with the website, which is useful for a number of different purposes.

## Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies to enhance your experience with our Online AI SaaS Subscription Service:

1. **Session Cookies:** These cookies are temporary and expire after your session ends.
2. **Persistent Cookies:** These cookies stay on your computer until you delete them. They are used to remember information that you have entered, such as your login details or items added to your shopping cart.
3. **Analytical Cookies:** These cookies collect information about how you use our Service, such as the pages you visit and the links you click on.
4. **Advertising Cookies:** These cookies collect information about your browsing habits to enable us to show you targeted advertisements.

## Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience with our Service. For example, we use cookies to:

* Remember your login details
* Show you targeted advertisements
* Analyze how you use our Service

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can usually manage your cookie preferences in your web browser settings. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our Service though your access to some functionality and areas of our Service may be restricted.
