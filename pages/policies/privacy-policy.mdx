# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024 \
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024

## Welcome to Gradison!

At Gradison, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled with care. This Privacy Policy describes how Gradison ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our Platform (the "Service").

## The Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

When you sign up for our Service, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. Additionally, when you make a purchase, we collect your credit card information and billing address.

### Information We Collect Automatically

We automatically collect data about your usage of the Service, including your subscription type, usage history, and interactions with the features and functionalities of the Service.

### Information We Receive From Other Sources

We may receive additional information about you from other sources, such as social media platforms or other online services.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data we collect to:

* Provide, operate, and maintain our Service
* Improve, personalize, and expand our Service
* Understand and analyze how you use our Service
* Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
* Communicate with you, to provide you with updates and other information related to our Service
* Monitor and enforce our terms of use and other agreements
* Prevent fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity
* Debug and investigate technical issues

## How We Disclose Your Information

We may disclose your personal information in the following circumstances:

* With your consent
* To comply with legal obligations
* To enforce our terms of use and other agreements
* To protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our users, or others

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you use our Service, plus a reasonable amount of time for backup purposes.

## How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

## International Data Transfers

We may transfer your personal information outside of your country or region for storage or processing purposes. We will take all necessary measures to ensure that your data remains protected and in line with international data protection standards.

## Services Offered by Third Parties

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service, provide the Service on our behalf, perform Service-related services, or assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

## How to Control Your Privacy

You can control how we process your information, how to delete or correct certain information, and how to request access to your information. You can choose what communities to participate in and what information you add to our services. You can choose what messages to send or post, who to engage with, what information to include in your profile, whether to connect any third party services to your account, and more.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@gradison.ca.
