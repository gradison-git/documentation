# Gradison Security and Data Protection

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024 \
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024

At Gradison, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring that our platform is a secure environment for interaction and collaboration.

## Our Commitment to Security

Gradison employs a multi-layered approach to security, including:

- **Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) Encryption**: All data transmitted between our servers and users is encrypted using SSL, ensuring that sensitive information cannot be intercepted or tampered with.
- **Regular Updates and Patches**: We continuously update our platform and apply security patches promptly to address vulnerabilities and protect against known threats.
- **Strict Access Controls**: Our platform implements strict access controls to limit unauthorized access to user accounts and data.
- **User Authentication**: We use strong authentication methods, including two-factor authentication, to verify the identity of our users and prevent unauthorized access.
- **Data Backup and Recovery**: We regularly back up user data to prevent loss and have robust recovery processes in place in case of data breaches.

## User Privacy and Data Handling

Gradison is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users. We collect and store only the necessary data required to provide our services, and we adhere to the following principles regarding user data:

- **Minimal Data Collection**: We collect only the data necessary to provide our services and do not sell or share personal information with third parties without explicit consent.
- **Data Usage**: We use collected data solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services, and we do not use it for marketing or other commercial purposes.
- **User Consent**: We obtain clear and informed consent from users before collecting or processing their personal data.
- **Data Retention**: We retain user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill our obligations to users and comply with legal requirements.
- **Right to Erasure**: Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data under certain circumstances, and we process such requests in accordance with applicable laws.

## Incident Response and Reporting

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we have a dedicated incident response team that works swiftly to mitigate the threat and restore normal operations. We also encourage users to report any suspicious activity or potential security issues to our security team.

## Compliance with Laws and Standards

Gradison complies with all applicable data protection and privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States. We are committed to transparency and provide clear information about our data collection and usage practices to our users.

## Your Role in Security

Users play a crucial role in maintaining the security of their accounts. We encourage users to:

- Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Regularly update their password and review their account settings.
- Be wary of phishing attempts and never share sensitive information via email or unsecured messaging apps.
